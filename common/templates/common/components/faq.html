General

Blender Studio is a Film and Training publishing platform for everything produced by the Studio team in the Blender Institute.

The money Blender Studio makes through subscriptions is used to keep our services running, and to make open source and open content productions possible. Throughout the production cycle our team produce training, rigs and assets for publishing to Blender Studio subscribers.

The Blender Studio funds the Blender Studio team, who rigorously test and use Blender in the Open Movie productions. The team work with core Blender Developers at the Blender Institute to plan, design and test core Blender features.
