Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions about the Cloud, please contact one of the team on Blender Chat by logging in with your Blender ID.

General

Blender Cloud is a Film and Training publishing platform for everything produced by the Studio team in the Blender Institute.
The money Blender Cloud makes through subscriptions is used to keep our services running, and to make open source and open content productions possible. Throughout the production cycle our team produce training, rigs and assets for publishing to Blender Cloud subscribers.
The Blender Cloud funds the Blender Studio team, who rigorously test and use Blender in the Open Movie productions. The team work with core Blender Developers at the Blender Institute to plan, design and test core Blender features.

Payment & Billing

Blender Cloud subscriptions are available for purchase through the Blender Store.

The Blender Store offers payment through major credit cards, PayPal or direct bank transfer.
You may delete your account at any time via your personal account page, or by contacting us at cloudsupport@blender.org.

In case of suspected fraud or abuse of the service after registration and membership payment, the account owner will be contacted and asked for clarification. Failure to alleviate charges of fraud may result in the termination of the member’s account.

Upon termination, all licenses granted by the Blender Cloud will terminate. In the event of account deletion for any reason, content that you submitted may no longer be available. The Blender Cloud shall not be responsible for the loss of such content.
Membership fees and supporter contributions are non-refundable. That money keeps our services running, and is used to make open source and open content productions possible.
