Settlers

A gift from the Blender Animation Studio team to the community. Free character rigs and sets.

Settlers is a collection of character rigs, environments and animated scenes, created by the Blender Animation Studio team and shared with the community as they are made. Completely free, for everyone. Download all the assets and create your own animations!
